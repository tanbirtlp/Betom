about us

Better Tomorrow (BeTom) is a Sweden based non-profit charitable development organization. It is registered as a non-profit organization under Swedish Tax authority. All activities and financial transaction of this organization is monitored by Swedish Social welfare ministry. It is necessary to submit yearly activities and financial report of the organization to the responsible authority. The organization was officially formed in 2012 at Stockholm, the capital city of Sweden, but the organizers were doing activities since long time before it was shaped as a formal structure. Currently, it works jointly with many local and international organizations in Sweden. The organization runs completely in voluntary basis. There is no paid stuff working for this organization.

Education Aid

BeTom believes that to ensure sustainable development of an underprivileged community, necessity of education is must. Education is the road to know how people can gain their full credibility in life. We are more interested to support someone to earn some skills so that he or she can become self sufficient by investing that skills. Though education is one of the basic needs of human being, yet there are many underprivileged communities around the world who don’t have good access to get education. Better Tomorrow has agenda to support students to get quality education. We offer monthly scholarship to the students who are helpless and cannot afford study costs. Students from primary education to university level are considered for this scholarship program. Our volunteers find those students and they continuously monitor study progress of the selected students.

Emergency Aid

Better Tomorrow always come forward to help people in emergency situations. Our volunteers try to reach to the victims of different natural calamities like flood, severe winter or sudden accident with life saving aids. We feel heartily how a family can become helpless due to a sudden accident or natural calamities. Our volunteers not only give relief support to the victims, but they also try to support mental strength of the victims. We follow up with them to give them necessary rehabilitation.

Smile for All

We believe that everyone has the right to enjoy life whether the person is poor or rich. What we see around us rich people enjoy their very gorgeously, while poor people stay away. Even in different festivals rich people celebrates with new cloths, plenty of delicious food and enjoy many other events of pleasure. The underprivileged communities of the society remains deprived of enjoying those festivals. They can’t afford new clothes or good food in those days. Better Tomorrow dreams to ensure everyone is smiling at in the days of their religious or regional festivals. Our volunteers knock to the door of those people with new cloths, good food and gifts in different festivals. Our pleasure knows no bounds when we see a young kid is smiling after wearing a new cloth.
